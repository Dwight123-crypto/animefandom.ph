Quick Guide to Defeating a Titan - Levi's Style

In the world of "Attack on Titan," Titans are colossal, humanoid creatures that pose a significant threat to humanity. This guide will provide you with a quick strategy inspired by Levi's battle against the Beast Titan.

Remember, defeating a Titan requires immense skill, agility, and careful planning.

  1. Equip Yourself
  2. Analyze the Situation
  3. Exploit the Titan's Blind Spots

You can learn more at IGN Southeast Asia.

Equip Yourself

ODM Gear: Obtain the ODM (Vertical Maneuvering Equipment) gear, essential for quick movement and aerial combat.

  • Dual Blades: Arm yourself with two blades, which are ideal for slicing through Titan flesh.
  • Assess the Titan's size, strength, and behavior. Identify any unique characteristics or weaknesses.
  • Understand the terrain: Take note of the surrounding structures, obstacles, and potential hiding spots.
  • Titans have vulnerable areas at the nape of their necks, which is their main weak point.
  • Utilize speed and agility to maneuver around the Titan swiftly, constantly targeting its blind spots.
